Les 400 Coups Branding + Packaging

Branding and Packaging Design for a craft brewery called “Les 400 Coups”. The french idiom “faire Les 400 Coups” means “to raise hell”. The logo reflects that meaning through the use of a “shattered” character. The visual style of the packaging was built around that pattern, incorporating it in different variations to create a strong and coherent brand identity.
